ABOUT

Hello! My name is Ana Catarina. I’m (almost) 28 y.o and (among

many other things) I’m a designer(?).


Born in V. N. Gaia, I studied and graduated from Design+Multimedia

bachelors and (almost) masters degree in Coimbra (FCTUC) - class of 2023.


I love to cook for my friends & family, to draw, to try new things (knitting,

clay, whatever), to sleep on the couch while something’s on tv, to take

analog pictures, to drink wine and to do/look at collages.


I learned that design, much like collages, can gatter all kinds of processes into

one solution. It can be coded, it can be drawn, photographed (...)

- it can be many things. And I believe that most of great solutions lie on this

multidisciplinary area.


I also believe my dog Molly is the cutest dog on earth.
